Discover our different types of espresso available for purchase.
Each made fresh by the order allowing you to enjoy not only the aroma but the crisp flavor of a hot cup made especially for you.

Coffee Description Price
Espresso An Espresso is a concentrated form of coffee served in small, strong shots $2.10
Latte A latte is a coffee drink with espresso, steamed milk and a layer of foam on top. It has ⅓ espresso and 2/3 steamed milk, with a thin layer of foam on top $3.50
Mocha A mocha or mocha latte is a drink made with espresso and steamed milk that's flavored with chocolate syrup. $4.00
Cappuccino A cappuccino is an espresso drink with steamed milk, milk foam and espresso. It has equal parts espresso, steamed milk and foam (1/3 each) $3.00
Americano An Americano is an espresso drink made with hot water and espresso. The drink can be made with either one or two shots of espresso, and varying ratios of water (usually 2:1) $2.70
Macchiato A caffè macchiato or espresso macchiato is a shot of espresso that's topped with frothed milk. $2.30
Flavor Shot Need to change it up? Add some flavor to your coffee with any of these: Vanilla, Cinnamon, White Chocolate, Pumpkin Spice, Brown Sugar $0.50
